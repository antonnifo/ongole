Know About Our Farm
And History

Ongole Beef was established in 2019 and began its operations in 2020 with a herd of 30 cattle.The herd and farm capacity has grown exponentially and currently the farm has over 3,000 steers. The Farm is located in Laikipia county in the Semi-arid areas of the central rift-valley overlooking Mt Kenya and the Aberdare ranges.We source our cattle from the neighboring local communities who are predominantly pastoralists and have practiced the craft for centuries. This ensures that the stock is adaptive to the local climate, pests and diseases. We practice the Feedlot system whereby the steers are fed on our special feed formulations to enable them attain the desired harvest weight within three months. Most of these feeds are grown at the farm. Besides beef cattle production, Ongole Beef also rears goats and sheep.
