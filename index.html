Know About Our Farm
And History

The Farm is located in Laikipia county in the Semi-arid areas of the central rift-valley overlooking Mt Kenya and the Aberdare ranges.We source our cattle from the neighboring local communities who are predominantly pastoralists and have practiced the craft for centuries. This ensures that the stock is adaptive to the local climate, pests and diseases. We practice the Feedlot system whereby the steers are fed on our special feed formulations to enable them attain the desired harvest weight within three months. Most of these feeds are grown at the farm. Besides beef cattle production, Ongole Beef also rears goats and sheep.

Superior Bulls

As we endeavor to improve on quality of our herd, we have started cattle cross-breeding programs. This involves introducing well selected pure breed bulls to our cows. This programs objective is to have hybrid offspring that will yield more and higher quality beef. After calving, our cows are not milked and this ensures that our calves feed enough which make them grow fast and have good health.
